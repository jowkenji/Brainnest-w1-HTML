What is Woodwork?

Woodworking is the skill of making items from wood, and includes:

  • Cabinet making (cabinetry and furniture)
  • Wood carving
  • Joinery
  • Carpentry
  • Woodturning

Tools

You're gonna need some tools to work the wood:

  • Hand Tools
  • Power Tools

Materials

In modern Woodworking, you can also use several types of materials, mechanical and eletronic parts.
